Proposed Additional Data Requests for Google Travel Insights

Department of Culture and Tourism - Abu Dhabi | May 7, 2025

  • Home

Executive Summary

Based on our analysis of Google's current travel reports, we've identified several high-value data points and research questions that would significantly enhance Abu Dhabi's tourism strategy. These additional insights would allow us to better understand traveler behavior, refine our targeting, and optimize our marketing investments across key markets.

The following proposal outlines specific data requests that leverage Google's unique search, YouTube, and travel insights capabilities to provide Abu Dhabi with actionable intelligence for tourism development and marketing.

1. Competitive Positioning

Data Request Strategic Value Application
Search volume comparison between Abu Dhabi and key competitors (Dubai, Doha, Riyadh) Understand our relative market position Identify competitive gaps and opportunities
Share of search analysis for Middle Eastern destinations Track Abu Dhabi's mindshare over time Measure effectiveness of awareness campaigns
Comparative destination perception attributes Understand our strengths and weaknesses vs. competitors Refine our unique value proposition

2. Market-Specific Insights

Data Request Strategic Value Application
Detailed search behavior analysis for our top 10 source markets Understand market-specific interests and motivations Create more targeted marketing campaigns
Rising search terms related to Abu Dhabi by market Identify emerging interests and trends Develop timely, relevant content
Cross-destination search patterns (which destinations are researched alongside Abu Dhabi) Understand our competitive set from travelers' perspective Position Abu Dhabi more effectively against alternatives

3. Digital Content Effectiveness

Data Request Strategic Value Application
YouTube engagement metrics for Abu Dhabi travel content by market and demographic Understand what content resonates with different audiences Optimize video content strategy
Content themes driving highest engagement (culture, luxury, family, adventure) Identify most compelling aspects of Abu Dhabi's offering Align content creation with traveler interests
Creator impact analysis for Middle East travel content Understand influence of content creators on destination selection Inform influencer partnership strategy

4. Travel Planning Behavior

Data Request Strategic Value Application
Detailed booking window analysis for Abu Dhabi by market Understand when travelers are making decisions Optimize campaign timing and messaging
Multi-destination trip analysis (how Abu Dhabi fits into larger itineraries) Identify partnership opportunities with complementary destinations Develop multi-destination packages and marketing
Stopover traveler behavior and conversion analysis Understand potential of stopover market Develop targeted stopover campaigns

5. Value Proposition Testing

Data Request Strategic Value Application
Price sensitivity analysis by market and traveler segment Understand willingness to pay for Abu Dhabi experiences Optimize pricing and promotional strategies
Feature importance analysis (what attributes drive destination selection) Identify which aspects of Abu Dhabi to emphasize Refine messaging hierarchy by market
Barrier analysis for considering Abu Dhabi as a destination Understand what's preventing visitation Address misconceptions and concerns in marketing
